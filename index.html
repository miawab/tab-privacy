Privacy Policy

Last updated · June 2025

Tab is a bill-splitting calculator. We designed it to work with as little data collection as possible. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how.

01

Information We Collect

Tab collects the minimum necessary to operate the app.

  • Camera images — only when you use the receipt scanner. Images are sent directly to our processing service and are never stored, logged, or retained after the scan completes.
  • Anonymous session ID — a random string generated locally each time the app launches (e.g. user_k3x9mq2p1). This is used solely to enforce free-tier scan rate limits and is not linked to your identity.
  • Purchase status — if you upgrade to Pro ✨ Pro, your purchase is verified through Google Play and managed by RevenueCat. We receive only an entitlement status (pro: yes/no) — no payment details ever touch our servers.

We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, device identifiers, or any other personal information.

02

How We Use Your Information

  • Receipt images are passed to Google Gemini AI to extract line items. The image is used for this single request and immediately discarded.
  • Session IDs are checked against our rate-limit counter in Supabase to enforce the daily scan limit for free users. No other use.
  • Purchase status is used to unlock Pro features (AI scanning, no ads) and enforce scan caps appropriate to your plan.

We do not use any data for advertising profiling, analytics, or any purpose beyond making the app function.

03

Third-Party Services

Tab uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • Supabase — hosts our edge function that proxies receipt scans and enforces rate limits. Data is processed in Supabase's infrastructure and subject to their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini AI — processes receipt images to extract text. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Images are not retained by Gemini for model training under our usage tier.
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app purchase entitlements. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy. RevenueCat does not receive your receipt images.
  • Google AdMob — serves banner ads to free-tier users only. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad serving per Google's Privacy Policy. Ads are not shown to Pro users.

04

Data Retention

  • Receipt images — zero retention. Not stored anywhere after the scan response is returned.
  • Session IDs & scan counts — stored in Supabase for up to 30 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Purchase records — retained by RevenueCat and Google Play per their respective policies for legal and financial compliance.

05

Children's Privacy

Tab is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

06

Your Rights

Because Tab collects no personal information tied to your identity, there is no profile to access, correct, or delete. Your anonymous session ID resets every time you reinstall the app.

If you have questions about data held by our third-party providers (e.g. RevenueCat purchase history), please refer to their respective privacy policies linked above.

07

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
